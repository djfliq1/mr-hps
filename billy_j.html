The Legend

Billy J. Ganaway

1933-2013


Billy J. Ganaway was raised in Kansas City, MO. Joined the Air Force where he served honorably for 20 years. He moved to Colorado Springs, raised his family and retired from the Air Force. As a God loving man, he walked his life with honor, wisdom, determination, and conviction.

Though he will most be remembered by his brilliant smile, humor and the love he gave for all he came to know.
